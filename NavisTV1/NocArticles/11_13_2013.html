According to Alex Becce, Terminal and Commercial Manager at DP World Jeddah, “The Navis Optimization Modules of Expert Decking and PrimeRoute have helped us to dramatically improve the productivity of our operations. Our customers have experienced an improvement in service and we have been able to reduce our operating costs as a result.”

Located on the Red Sea, DP World Jeddah serves as a vital link between the world's busy Europe and Asia trade routes. Recognizing the inevitable challenges described above, the terminal put an aggressive plan in place to improve its operations. Key aspects focused on advancing the visibility of operations, improving overall systems management and reporting reliability. In addition, motivating the work force and improving accountability, teamwork and safety has also been key operational goals.

The specific benefit of PrimeRoute allowed DP World Jeddah to improve its productivity by reducing the travel distance for unladen ITV’s and to increase laden travel. This productivity improvement decreases fuel consumption, while increasing the number of jobs per shift hour, congestion control and visibility of operations. The key benefit of expert decking helped DP World Jeddah better manage general cargo allocations and increase the flexibility of its system.
