It is my pleasure to announce that Generale de Manutention Portuaire (GMP) - Le Havre successfully went live with N4 v2.3.7.3 at Terminal North on Sunday, 02 November 2013 with Vessel, Yard and Rail Operations. Gate Operations commenced on Monday, 03 November 2013.

As our first customer in France which pose unique challenges, it’s taken some time and a lot of effort to reach this point. Navis SN4 has handled GMP’s normal Operational volumes from Day One with 0 serious issues. There was no drop in operational productivity after startup. GMP is already achieving same productivity and Gate turnaround times as before.
